Homework 9: Choropleth of NCNHP Natural Areas

Lauren Hall

For this homework, I wanted to take a look at which counties in North Carolina had the most Natural Area acreage. A Natural Heritage Program Natural Area is a terrestrial or aquatic area that has special biodiversity significance, such as the presence of a rare species, high-quality natural communities, important animal assemblages, or other ecological features, collectively known as “Elements” of biodiversity. This program is designed to identify these areas and enact measures to protect them.

This map uses the Natural Breaks classifying method. I used this because I wanted the data to be grouped in similar groups to display the trends best. I also cleaned up the values to make it slightly easier to read.
